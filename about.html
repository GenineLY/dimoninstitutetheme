about the dimon institute

Founded in 1997, The Dimon Institute is a world renowned center for the study of awareness and movement science, and is dedicated to creating to a culture of excellence, developing the field of Psychophysical Education, and preparing highly effective teachers and leaders to make a difference in real-world settings.

Students from around the world come to The Dimon Institute for professional training and certification. The Institute offers a two-year program leading to certification in Sensorimotor Awareness, training for professionals to develop advanced and specialized skills, and is open to the public for virtual and on-site classes.

Mission

The mission of The Dimon Institute is to teach the art and science of self-awareness. We are pioneering the study of mind and body as a distinct field of knowledge that transforms our understanding of child development, learning, and health. The Institute provides an innovative pathway for the integrated exploration of mind and body, offering a groundbreaking field of knowledge that reshapes traditional learning and fosters deeper understanding of human potential.

Vision

Our vision is the establishment of the study of mind and body as a new field of knowledge that applies to child development, learning, and health. We aim to establish a future where this integrated approach serves as a foundational element of human growth, enriching lives and deepening our understanding of human potential.

Values

  • Love of Knowledge
  • • Excellence in Education •
  • Student-Centered

Love of knowledge and desire to widen our understanding of the field of psychophysical education. Offering students a supportive learning environment while maintaining an uncompromising standard of excellence. Searching for ways of teaching that respect and support the student’s learning process and recognize the challenges of learning.
