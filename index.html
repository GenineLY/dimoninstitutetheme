the school for awareness and movement science

The essence of the Dimon Institute program is founded on the understanding that each of us possesses the capacity to become fully conscious in thought and action. Students from around the world come to the Institute for professional training and certification in the disciplines we study.

virtual courses & online resources

Our programs are fully accessible online, allowing you to study at your own pace. Engage with expert-led materials and interactive sessions designed to deepen your understanding and application of these powerful concepts from anywhere in the world.

on-site classes & training

The Dimon Institute is open to the public for on-site sessions which provide an immersive experience in the study of movement and awareness sciences. In this vibrant, supportive setting, you'll work directly with experts, gaining hands-on skills and insights.

alumni support

We're building a strong alumni network through online platforms and in-person events, fostering meaningful connections. Virtual communities provide ongoing discussions while in-person gatherings allow for deeper relationships. Together, we'll create an impactful, supportive community.
